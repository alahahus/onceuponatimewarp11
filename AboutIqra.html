Iqra Osman


Iqra Osman is a fourth-year Educational Studies (BA) student whose interest in education spills into everything she does. With a desire to shift our current relationships with learning and how Canadians choose to define success, she finds herself constantly interrogating the principles that uphold the status quo. In confronting storytelling and our prescribed expectations of colonialism as a signifier of 'correctness', Iqra, alongside her teammates, hopes that the status quo of storytelling can also be challenged. For this project, she puts to use her research skills as well as her broader understanding of education-related knowledge (ie. teaching resources, curriculum analysis). Additionally, Iqra's experience in working with young students informs how OUATW approaches their greater solution- in a way that is mindful of those who will be most impacted. Once Upon A Time Warp has allowed Iqra to merge her passions of education, advocacy, and community empowerment into a holistic project that enables authentic expression and connection.
