Rachel Clodman


Rachel Clodman is a fourth year student majoring in Educational Studies and minoring in Psychology. She is also currently completing her Bachelor of Education in the Primary/ Junior division. Additionally, Rachel is the current president of the faculty of education student association at York university. She is interested in improving literacy education for children and helping students rethink the meaning of storytelling. Rachel is very glad to have been part of this team and hopes it will lead to great improvements in the field of education.
