Shibli Sajeed


Shibli Sajeed is a fourth-year student, who is currently completing his Bachelor of Arts (Honors) in International Development Studies. He is a member of the Model UN, the Golden Key International Honor Society, and the Track & Field Club at York University. He is interested in promoting environmental awareness and social justice. For the C4 course, Shibli has diligently applied his analytical skills to learn how the project in this course can help students have a better understanding of other cultures. Shibli is hopeful this research will lead to a deeper understanding of diversity in classrooms.
